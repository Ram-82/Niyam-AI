The compliance challenge facing Indian businesses

Every year, thousands of MSMEs face penalties, lose ITC credits, and struggle with manual compliance processes that drain time and money.

Complexity overload

GST, TDS, ROC, Income Tax — each has its own portal, rules, and deadlines. Keeping track of everything manually is nearly impossible.

Missed deadlines

A single missed filing date can trigger penalties of up to Rs 10,000 per return. Most businesses only realize after the damage is done.

Lost ITC credits

Mismatches between purchase records and GSTR-2B mean businesses regularly lose lakhs in legitimate Input Tax Credits.

Manual dependency

Relying on spreadsheets, WhatsApp messages, and disconnected tools leads to errors, duplication, and communication gaps.
